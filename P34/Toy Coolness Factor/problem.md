## Title

Toy Coolness Factor

## Slug

toy-coolness-factor

## Difficulty

Medium

## Description

A toy factory produces $n$ types of toy cars and $n$ types of paint, both ranked from 1 to $n$. The "coolness factor" of a toy car of type $i$ with paint type $j$ is $i \times j$.The design team wants to find the median coolness factor from all $n^2$ combinations to plan their new product line. Given $n$ is an odd number, what is the median?

## Examples

### 1

#### Input

3

#### Output

3

#### Explanation

The numbers in increasing order are [1,2,2,3,3,4,6,6,9], so the answer is 3. 
    
### 2

#### Input

5

#### Output

8

#### Explanation
The middle element is 8.
