## Title

Experimental Forest Growth

## Slug

experimental-forest-growth

## Difficulty

Medium

## Description

An experimental forest is laid out in an $n \times n$ grid (where $n$ is odd). The rows and columns are numbered 1 to $n$. A special tree planted at the intersection of row $i$ and column $j$ is projected to grow to a height of $i \times j$ meters.Scientists want to find the median projected height of all $n^2$ trees in the forest.

## Examples

### 1

#### Input

3

#### Output

3

#### Explanation

The numbers in increasing order are [1,2,2,3,3,4,6,6,9], so the answer is 3. 
    
### 2

#### Input

5

#### Output

8

#### Explanation
The middle element is 8.
