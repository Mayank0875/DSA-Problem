## Title

Wizard's Spell Force

## Slug

wizards-spell-force

## Difficulty

Medium

## Description

A wizard is practicing magic. She has $n$ wands, ranked 1 to $n$, and $n$ spellbooks, also ranked 1 to $n$. When she uses wand $i$ with spellbook $j$, the resulting spell has a magical force of $i \times j$.She wants to find the median force value among all $n^2$ possible combinations to understand her average power. Given that $n$ is an odd number, find this median.

## Examples

### 1

#### Input

3

#### Output

3

#### Explanation

The numbers in increasing order are [1,2,2,3,3,4,6,6,9], so the answer is 3. 
    
### 2

#### Input

5

#### Output

8

#### Explanation
The middle element is 8.
