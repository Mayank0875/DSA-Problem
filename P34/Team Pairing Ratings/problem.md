## Title

Team Pairing Ratings

## Slug

team-pairing-ratings

## Difficulty

Medium

## Description

A sports league has $n$ "home" teams and $n$ "away" teams, each ranked from 1 to $n$. When home team $i$ plays against away team $j$, the match is given an "excitement rating" of $i \times j$.A sports analyst wants to find the median excitement rating for all $n^2$ possible matchups to write an article. Given that $n$ is odd, help the analyst find this value.

## Examples

### 1

#### Input

3

#### Output

3

#### Explanation

The numbers in increasing order are [1,2,2,3,3,4,6,6,9], so the answer is 3. 
    
### 2

#### Input

5

#### Output

8

#### Explanation
The middle element is 8.
