## Title

Monumental Years

## Slug

monumental-years

## Difficulty

Medium

## Description

A historian is studying a period of history from year L to year R. She considers a year "monumental" if its number is a perfect square. Help her count the number of monumental years in this range.

Do not use any built-in square root or power functions.


## Examples

### 1

#### Input

9 25

#### Output

3

#### Explanation

The perfect squares in the range [9, 25] are 9 (3x3), 16 (4x4), and 25 (5x5). There are 3 such numbers.

### 2

#### Input

1 10

#### Output

3

#### Explanation

The perfect squares in the range [1, 10] are 1 (1x1), 4 (2x2), and 9 (3x3). There are 3 such numbers.

## Input Format

The input consists of a single line with two space-separated integers, L and R.

## Output Format

- Return a single integer representing the count of perfect squares in the inclusive range [L, R].

## Constraints

- 1 ≤ L ≤ R ≤ 10^18

## Time Limit

2 second

## Memory Limit

256 MB
