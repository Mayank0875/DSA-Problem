## Title

Chocolate Tastiness Score

## Slug

chocolate-tastiness-score

## Difficulty

Medium

## Description

A chocolate factory makes $n$ types of chocolate and $n$ types of fillings, both ranked by quality from 1 to $n$. The "tastiness score" of a candy made with chocolate $i$ and filling $j$ is $i \times j$.The factory's head taster wants to find the median tastiness score from all $n^2$ possible combinations. Given that $n$ is an odd integer, calculate this median.

## Examples

### 1

#### Input

3

#### Output

3

#### Explanation

The numbers in increasing order are [1,2,2,3,3,4,6,6,9], so the answer is 3. 
    
### 2

#### Input

5

#### Output

8

#### Explanation
The middle element is 8.
