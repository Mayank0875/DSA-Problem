## Title

Salesperson Revenue

## Slug

salesperson-revenue

## Difficulty

Medium

## Description

A company has $n$ salespeople (ranked 1 to $n$) and $n$ products (ranked 1 to $n$). The revenue generated by salesperson $i$ selling product $j$ is known to be $i \times j$.The sales manager wants to find the median revenue value from all $n^2$ salesperson-product pairings to set future goals. Given that $n$ is odd, find the median revenue.

## Examples

### 1

#### Input

3

#### Output

3

#### Explanation

The numbers in increasing order are [1,2,2,3,3,4,6,6,9], so the answer is 3. 
    
### 2

#### Input

5

#### Output

8

#### Explanation
The middle element is 8.
